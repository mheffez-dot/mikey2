What is the CPS Vendor Validator?

Upload a list of vendor websites, and instantly determine which ones sell products qualified for CPS extended warranties.

How It Works

  • Smart Crawling: Fetches pages using direct fetch or advanced unblockers for bot-protected sites.
  • AI Classification: Utilizes DeepSeek to analyze page titles, descriptions, and menus against the CPS coverage policy (Covered: electronics, appliances, tools, firearms; Excluded: apparel, food, services, software).
  • Detailed Verdicts: Delivers a Pass/Fail/Mixed verdict, confidence score, category mapping, and quoted evidence.

What You Receive

  • Automated Google Sheet logging (one row per vendor) + a run summary.
  • Email delivery with the comprehensive results CSV attached.
  • Duplicate skipping to save time and API costs.
  • High-speed parallel processing (~20 sites at a time) at fractions of a cent per site.
