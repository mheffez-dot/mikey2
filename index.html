What is the CPS Vendor Validator?

In one line: You give it a list of vendor websites, and it tells you which ones sell products CPS can write extended warranties on.

How It Works

1
Fetch: Retrieves the website (free direct fetch first, Bright Data's unblocker for bot-protected sites).
2
Read: Analyzes the page — title, description, and product/category menu.
3
Classify: Uses DeepSeek AI against CPS coverage policy (covered = powered/durable high-ticket goods like electronics, appliances, tools, firearms; excluded = apparel, food, services, software, etc.).
4
Record: Generates a verdict (Pass / Fail / Mixed) with confidence score, matched category, real quoted evidence, and a one-line reason.

What You Get

  • Results written to a Google Sheet (one row per vendor) + a per-run summary
  • An email with the results CSV attached
  • Automatic duplicate skipping (won't re-check or re-bill domains already processed)

Processing Details: Upload a CSV of domains via this form. The system processes ~20 sites at a time in parallel at approximately $0.002/site (often less since easy sites skip Bright Data). Any provider outages are flagged automatically.

Bottom Line: Replaces hours of manually visiting vendor sites and judging warrantability — turning a list of domains into a scored, evidence-backed Pass/Fail sheet in minutes.
