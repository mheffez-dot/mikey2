CPS Vendor Validator

Automated vendor warrantability screening

What It Does

You give it a list of vendor websites, and it tells you which ones sell products CPS can write extended warranties on.

How It Works

For each domain you submit:

  • Fetches the website — Direct fetch first, with Bright Data's unblocker for bot-protected sites
  • Reads the page — Extracts title, description, and product/category menu
  • Classifies with AI — Uses DeepSeek against your CPS coverage policy (covered = powered/durable high-ticket goods like electronics, appliances, tools, firearms; excluded = apparel, food, services, software, etc.)
  • Records a verdict — Pass / Fail / Mixed, plus confidence score, matched category, real quoted evidence, and a one-line reason

What You Get

  • Results written to a Google Sheet (one row per vendor) + a per-run summary
  • An email with the results CSV attached
  • Automatic duplicate skipping (won't re-check or re-bill domains already processed)

Processing Details

Upload a CSV of domains via the form below. It processes ~20 sites at a time in parallel at ~$0.002/site (often less since easy sites skip Bright Data). Provider outages are flagged automatically. Replaces hours of manual site visits with scored, evidence-backed Pass/Fail results in minutes.
